âš–ï¸Welcome to NTU AIS Lab

Welcome to the National Taiwan University Artificial Intelligence Security Lab. Our research centers on trustworthy machine learning, focusing on robustness, privacy, and fairness. To bridge the current trustworthiness gap in ML, it is essential to address these interconnected challenges within a cohesive framework. This involves developing solutions that consider their complex interdependencies, aiming to create reliable, secure, and equitable machine learning systems, thereby fostering greater public trust in the technology.

We are always looking for highly-motivated undergraduate and graduate students. Please contact us if you are interested in our research.

âš–ï¸FAQ

  • Q: How can I join the NTU AIS Lab?
    A: We welcome self-motivated students, including undergraduates and graduates, to join us. If you are interested in the research topics of the lab, please email Professor Chen and include a brief resume. Professor Chen will arrange a short interview.
  • Q: What are the research areas of the NTUAIS Lab?
    A: Our research focuses on trustworthy machine learning, including robustness, privacy, and fairness.
  • Q: How can I learn more about the NTU AIS Lab's research?
    A: You can explore our publications and projects on this website.
  • Q: I am an undergraduate student. When is the best time for me to join the lab for a project?
    A: The sooner, the better.
  • Q: What qualities or skills does the professor expect from students working on their projects?
    A: The professor expects students to have a genuine desire to learn, be proactive rather than passive, and actively explore, with sufficient background in linear algebra and probability, and ideally, having completed a course in machine learning.
